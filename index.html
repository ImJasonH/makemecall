We all know we should call our representatives in Congress.

You might have even looked up their phone number. But now what? Calling people is soooo hard. I don't know about you, I'd rather use my phone to look at cat pictures.

What if they called you? That's where Make Me Call comes in.

Text JOIN and your zip code to 646-791-3269 to start.

Once you've signed up, you'll receive a call from us every day. When you pick up, you'll be automatically connected to one of your members of Congress at random.

Tell them what's on your mind, what issues you support or oppose.

You'll get a heads-up text five minutes before your call. You can text SKIP if now's not a good time and you'll get another call tomorrow.

Text NOW to do your call right now.

Text TIPS to get tips for your call.

Text QUIT any time to stop getting calls.
